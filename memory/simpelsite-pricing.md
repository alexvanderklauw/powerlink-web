## Psychology Breakdown

### The Decoy Effect
Growth is positioned as "Most Popular" to guide people there. Starter exists to make Growth look like a deal. Premium exists for those who want "the best."

### The Pain of Paying
- **One-time â‚¬500** = painful (wallet feels it)
- **â‚¬25/month** = painless (subscription fatigue already exists)
- Direct debit = they don't even see the money leave

### Loss Aversion
- "Cancel anytime" reduces commitment fear
- But ongoing value (updates, hosting) makes them stay
- "Your website goes offline if you cancel" = implied, not stated

### The Rule of 3
- Starter: "I might need more"
- Growth: "This feels right"
- Premium: "Maybe if I grow"
