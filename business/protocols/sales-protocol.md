## 7. Tone & Language Guidelines

### DO:
- Use local specifics (street names, competitors, neighborhoods)
- Ask questions that force quantification ("how many...")
- Be direct but respectful
- Use "jij/je" (informal) unless client uses "u"
- Keep it short (WhatsApp = mobile = short attention)

### DON'T:
- Use generic phrases like "I can help you grow"
- Pressure with false urgency ("only 2 spots left!")
- Write long paragraphs
- Apologize for reaching out ("sorry to bother you")
- Use technical jargon ("SEO-optimized responsive design")

## 8. The Competitor Research Rule

**Before contacting any business:**

1. **Find their competitors:** Google "[business type] [area]"
2. **Note the ones WITH websites:** These are your examples
3. **Note the ones WITHOUT:** These are your prospects
4. **Use specific names in your outreach:**
   - "[Competitor] on [Street] went online last week..."
   - "3 of your competitors already have websites..."

**Why this matters:** Generic claims are ignored. Specific claims are believed.
