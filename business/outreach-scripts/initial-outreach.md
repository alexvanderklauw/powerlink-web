## Quick Reference: Key Principles

### Loss Aversion
- People fear losing customers more than they value gaining features
- Always quantify the pain: "How many customers did you lose this month?"
- Anchor value to their number: "If just 1 of those 5 customers..."

### Neighbor Effect (Local Social Proof)
- Name specific competitors/streets: "[Business] on [Street] went live..."
- Create FOMO: "They have what you deserve"
- Use specific numbers: "already got 3 inquiries"

### Reframe "Cost" to "Investment"
- "You're paying â‚¬0 now... and getting â‚¬0 customers from Google"
- "What are 2 customers/year worth to you?"
- "Every week you wait = customers going to competitors"
